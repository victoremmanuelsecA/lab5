Music Genres

Afrobeat

Afrobeat is a music genre that involves the combination of west african musical styles and fuji music and highlife with American funk and jazz influnces, with a focus on chanted vocals,complex intersecting rhythms, and percussion.

Wizkid

Wizkid is a nigerian singer and songwriter known for his contributions to afrobeat music and his intertional collaborations.

P-square

p-square is a Nigerian music duo consisting of twin brothers peter and paul okeye. they are renowed for their afrobdeat and hip hop music.
